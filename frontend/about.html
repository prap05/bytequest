About MicroInvest

Investing for Everyone MicroInvest is a micro-investment platform designed especially for first-time investors across India.

The Problem We Solve Millions of people from rural, semi-urban, and low-income communities remain excluded from formal investing due to fear, lack of financial literacy, distrust, and the belief that investing requires large capital.

Our Mission To make investing simple, safe, and accessible by allowing users to start investing with as little as ₹10 in low-risk financial instruments.

User-First Design MicroInvest is built to work smoothly on low-end smartphones and limited internet connectivity, while educating users without overwhelming them.
